About Our Data

Our data includes speeches and public appearances from leaders across the United States, China, and Russia. Our dataset spans from 2005 to 2025, depending on the country, and provides comprehensive coverage of major diplomatic statements and addresses.

What You'll Find

Our platform provides tools to explore:

  • Keywords and Terms: The most frequently used concepts in diplomatic speeches
  • Named Entities: Countries, organizations, and key figures mentioned across speeches
  • Temporal Trends: How language and emphasis have evolved over time
  • Comparative Analysis: Differences in messaging across countries and leaders
  • Source Documents: Full text and context for every speech in our collection

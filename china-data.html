China Data

Dataset Overview

Our comprehensive database contains speeches and public statements from Chinese leadership spanning from 2005 to 2025. This collection provides authentic insights into how Chinese officials communicate on key geopolitical issues and domestic priorities.

Data Collection

We have gathered and analyzed over 1,000 speeches from the Chinese Foreign Ministry and other government officials. Each speech has been processed through natural language processing to extract entities, keywords, and thematic patterns that reveal evolving priorities in Chinese diplomatic discourse.

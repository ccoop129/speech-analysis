Why language matters

Governments communicate strategy not only through actions, but through words. Official speeches reveal how states frame power, security, and legitimacy—yet these signals are often buried in long texts and scattered across sources.

What this platform does

Geopolitical Speech Lab brings together over 1,000 official speeches from Russia, China, and the United States (2005–2025) and turns them into searchable, structured data. The platform enables users to track keywords, entities, and narrative shifts over time.

How to use it

Explore high-level insights or examine the data directly to form your own conclusions. The project aims to make great-power rhetoric easier to analyze, compare, and understand.

Welcome

What governments say—and don’t say—matters.

The Dataset

We gathered and analyzed 1000+ speeches made by leaders of the People's Republic of China from 2005 to 2025. We hope to empower policymakers, researchers, journalists, and students with an authentic, unpoliticized understanding of how the Chinese Communist Party communicates with the world and its citizens.

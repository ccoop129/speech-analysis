What this platform does

Geopolitical Speech Lab brings together over 1,000 official speeches from Russia and China (2005–2025) and turns them into searchable, structured data. The platform enables users to track keywords, entities, and narrative shifts over time.

How to use it

Explore high-level insights or examine the data directly to form your own conclusions. The project aims to make great-power rhetoric easier to analyze, compare, and understand.

The Dataset

We gathered and analyzed 1000+ speeches made by leaders of the United States, Russian Federation, and People's Republic of China from 2005 to 2025. We aim to empower policymakers, researchers, journalists, and students with an authentic, unpoliticized understanding of how official discourse evolves.
